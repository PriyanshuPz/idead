Fair Use Guidelines

TERMS.SUMMARY

These terms are a practical usage guide for Idead. They are intended to keep the graveyard readable, safe, and useful for everyone browsing abandoned ideas.

Acceptable Use

GUIDELINE.A

Do not post harmful, abusive, illegal, or malicious content. Keep submissions focused on project concepts and reflective context.

Intellectual Property

GUIDELINE.B

If you attach project links, make sure you have the right to share them and respect third-party ownership and licensing terms.

Public Visibility & Moderation

GUIDELINE.C

Ideas in the graveyard are visible to other visitors. Reporting exists to help maintain platform integrity. Ideas that cross moderation thresholds may be automatically removed.

This moderation flow is intentionally simple and designed for lightweight self-regulation of shared content.

Privacy Note

GUIDELINE.D

Idead operates without user accounts. It uses minimal anonymous interaction data for feature functionality, including rate limiting and view-count accuracy controls.
